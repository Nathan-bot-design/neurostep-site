About NeuroStep

NeuroStep is a smart rehabilitation system designed to support patients recovering from neurological conditions such as stroke. By combining sensor technology and remote connectivity, NeuroStep enables continuous physiotherapy beyond the hospital — bringing care directly into the patient’s home.

How It Works

Patient Uses Device

The patient wears NeuroStep during therapy sessions at home or in clinic.

Sensors Capture Data

Motion sensors track movement patterns and performance in real time.

Data Transmission

Information is sent securely via Bluetooth/WiFi to the connected platform.

Remote Monitoring

Physiotherapists monitor progress and provide guided feedback from anywhere.

Key Features

Real-time Movement Tracking

Precise sensors monitor lower-limb and gait patterns.

Adaptive Assistance

AI adjusts support levels based on patient performance.

Data-Driven Insights

Progress reports and analytics for better recovery decisions.

Designed for Africa

Portable, affordable, and built for low-resource settings.

Why NeuroStep Matters

Recovery Doesn't Stop

Patients continue high-quality therapy at home instead of losing progress after discharge.

Reduces Cost & Travel

Eliminates frequent expensive trips to clinics.

Remote Oversight

Physiotherapists stay connected and intervene early when needed.
